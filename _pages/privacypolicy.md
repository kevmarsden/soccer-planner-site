# Privacy Policy for Soccer Planner

Soccer Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Team and Player Data**: Names, positions, and game statistics you enter
- **Game Plans**: Lineup configurations and substitution plans you create
- **Settings**: Your preferences for game formats, formations, and timing

### Information We Don't Collect
- We do not collect personal contact information (email, phone numbers, addresses)
- We do not access your device's contacts, photos, or other personal data
- We do not track your location or use analytics services
- We do not collect any information about you personally

## How We Use Information

The information you enter is used solely to:
- Display your team rosters and lineup plans
- Calculate playing time and substitution schedules
- Save your game configurations and preferences
- Provide game timing and management features

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
- Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
- If you enable iCloud, your data may sync across your Apple devices
- This uses Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- You can disable iCloud sync in your device settings

### Data Backup
- The app creates local backups on your device for data recovery
- These backups remain on your device only
- No backups are sent to external services

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Your team and player information remains private to you.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
