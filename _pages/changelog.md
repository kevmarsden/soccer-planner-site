### `Initial Release`
# **Version 1.0**
Released September 17, 2025

The ultimate coaching tool for youth soccer teams is here! Soccer Planner makes it easy to manage fair playing time and strategic lineup planning for youth soccer coaches.

#### What's New
- Complete team and lineup management system
- Real-time game timer and substitution tracking
- Auto-balance algorithm for fair playing time
- Support for 7v7, 9v9, and 11v11 game formats
- Visual soccer field interface
- Local data storage with backup system
